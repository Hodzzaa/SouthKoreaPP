Understanding Mental Health in South Korea

Mental health issues have been a growing concern in South Korea. Despite significant economic growth and modernization, South Korea faces high rates of mental health disorders, including depression, anxiety, and high suicide rates.

Challenges

Social stigma, lack of awareness, and insufficient mental health resources are some of the challenges faced in addressing mental health issues in the country.

Support and Resources

Various organizations and government initiatives are working to improve mental health services and reduce the stigma associated with mental health issues in South Korea.
